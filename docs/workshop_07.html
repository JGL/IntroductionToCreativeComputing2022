This morning we are going to complete two exercises from Code as a Creative Medium, which was written by Tega Brain and Golan Levin. As I said yesterday, the day before and the day before that, I'd really recommend picking up a copy if you can, it's full of brilliant reference, exercises and project briefs.
This morning we are going to have 40 minutes for the first exercise, with 40 minutes for the second exercise.
A reminder from yesterday, the day before and the day before that: make these exercises on the online p5.js editor at https://editor.p5js.org - easier for sharing with others and a nice way of building up a portfolio of p5.js sketches.
Let's discuss "Butt Generator" before we all try it on our own on the p5.js editor. Any suggestions? This exercise was inspired by the following project by Le Wei: https://www.theatlantic.com/technology/archive/2013/11/the-bearable-lightness-of-buttgeneratorcom/281508/.
